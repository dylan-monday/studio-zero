### Full Description

Studio Zero is a custom-designed private studio at the corner of 18th and Valencia — the center of the Mission District, San Francisco's most vibrant neighborhood.

At approximately 300 square feet, the space is thoughtfully laid out with everything you need and nothing you don't: a comfortable queen bed, a dedicated workspace, high-speed WiFi, and a rain shower. There's shared garden access and laundry facilities on site.

The location is unbeatable. You're steps from some of the best coffee in the country, legendary taquerias, and the city's best bars. BART is a five-minute walk. Whether you're here for a short visit or a work trip, you'll feel like a local from day one.

**Note:** Studio Zero is a private residence in a residential building. We prioritize bookings from building tenants, though the space is occasionally available to others by arrangement.

## House Rules

### Welcome
Studio Zero is a private residence in a residential building. We're happy to share it with you — we just ask that you respect the space and the neighbors.

### Check-in & Check-out
- **Check-in:** 3:00 PM or later
- **Check-out:** 11:00 AM

Early check-in or late check-out may be available — just ask.

### Guests
- Maximum of 2 guests
- All guests must be listed on the reservation
- No unregistered overnight visitors

### Keys & Entry
- Access the building via the lockbox on the exterior (code provided before arrival)
- The studio is on the ground floor — look for the door with the "0" plaque
- A separate code unlocks the studio door
- **Important:** Please leave keys in the lockbox whenever you go out. This prevents lost keys and ensures you're never locked out.
- **$50 lost key fee** if keys are not returned to the lockbox at checkout

### The Space
- **No smoking** — inside or in the garden
- **No parties or events**
- **No pets** — we love them, but allergies don't
- Please remove shoes indoors
- No candles or incense (fire safety)
- **Heater:** Feel free to use it, but please turn it off before leaving and at checkout

### Being a Good Neighbor
This is a residential building with full-time tenants. Please be considerate:
- **Quiet hours:** 10 PM – 8 AM
- Keep noise to a reasonable level at all times
- Be especially quiet in hallways and shared spaces
- Keep garden visits brief and quiet — it's a shared space

### Garden & Laundry
- The garden is shared with building residents — please be respectful
- Washer and dryer are available in the backyard
- Keep garden use low-key and mindful of neighbors

### Amenities
- Extra towels are in the storage bench
- No coffee machine — but Four Barrel, Sightglass, and Ritual are all within a few blocks (you're welcome)

### WiFi
- **Network:** StudioZero
- **Password:** [included in check-in instructions]

### If Something Goes Wrong
Things happen. Just let us know right away — we'd rather fix it quickly than discover it later.

- **Property Manager (Steve):** steve@morleyfredericks.com / (415) 806-5455
- **Emergencies:** 911

### Cancellation Policy
- **30+ days before check-in:** Full refund (minus 3% processing fee)

Plans change — reach out and we'll see what we can do.

## Neighborhood Guide (for landing page)

### The Mission District

You're at the corner of 18th and Valencia — the heart of San Francisco's best neighborhood.

**Coffee**  
Some of the best coffee in the country is within walking distance.
- Linea Caffe (3417 18th St — literally across the street)
- Four Barrel Coffee (375 Valencia — 4 min walk)
- Ritual Coffee Roasters (1026 Valencia — 8 min walk)
- Sightglass Coffee (3014 20th St — 10 min walk)

**Food**  
- Flour + Water Pizzeria (Valencia & 18th — incredible pizza, right next door)
- La Taqueria (2889 Mission — legendary tacos, 5 min)
- Tartine Bakery (600 Guerrero — worth the line, 8 min)
- Delfina (3621 18th St — Cal-Italian staple, 2 min)
- Bi-Rite Market (3639 18th St — grab picnic supplies)
- Souvla (758 Valencia — fast-casual Greek)
- Tacolicious (741 Valencia — solid tacos + great margs)
- Lazy Bear (reservation required, tasting menu, splurge)

**Bars**  
- Trick Dog (3010 20th St — inventive cocktails, 8 min)
- ABV (3174 16th St — craft cocktails + food, 5 min)
- Beretta (1199 Valencia — cocktails + Italian, no reservations)
- Zeitgeist (199 Valencia — beer garden, cash only)
- El Rio (3158 Mission — dive bar + patio, 10 min)

**Sweets**  
- Bi-Rite Creamery (3692 18th St — ice cream, 3 min)
- Dandelion Chocolate (740 Valencia — bean-to-bar, 5 min)
- Craftsman and Wolves (746 Valencia — pastries, try "The Rebel Within")

**Parks**  
- Dolores Park (15 min walk — bring a blanket, great city views)
- Clarion Alley (between 17th & 18th — murals and street art)

**Getting Around**  
- BART: 16th St Mission (5 min walk)
- Muni: J Church, 14 Mission, 49 Van Ness
- Bike share: Bay Wheels stations on Valencia
- Uber/Lyft: You know the drill

**Valencia Street Note**  
Valencia goes car-free on weekend evenings (Fri 4pm–11pm, Sat 1pm–11pm) between 16th–21st — perfect for strolling, outdoor dining, and people-watching.

## FAQ (for FAQ page)

**Is there parking?**  
Street parking is available but can be competitive, especially on weekends. We recommend using BART (16th St Mission is a 5-minute walk) or rideshare.

**Is the studio private?**  
Yes — it's a completely private, self-contained unit with its own entrance. The garden and laundry are shared with building residents.

**How do I get in?**  
Full entry instructions (including lockbox code and door code) are sent 24 hours before check-in. Please read them carefully.

**Can I check in early or check out late?**  
Sometimes! Message us and we'll do our best to accommodate based on the booking schedule.

**Is there air conditioning?**  
San Francisco rarely needs it — the city stays cool year-round. The studio has a heater for cooler evenings (please turn it off when you leave).

**Is there a coffee maker?**  
No — but Linea Caffe is literally across the street, and Four Barrel, Ritual, and Sightglass are all within a short walk. You're in one of the best coffee neighborhoods in the country.

**Is there laundry?**  
Yes — washer and dryer are available in the backyard garden area.

**Is the neighborhood safe?**  
The Mission is a vibrant, busy neighborhood. Like anywhere in a city, stay aware of your surroundings, especially late at night. The studio is on a well-trafficked block at 18th & Valencia.

**Can I have visitors?**  
Unregistered guests are not allowed to stay overnight. If you'd like a friend to stop by during the day, that's fine — just be mindful of noise and remember this is a residential building.

**What about the garden?**  
The garden is shared with building residents. You're welcome to use it, but please keep visits brief and quiet out of respect for neighbors.

**What's the WiFi speed?**  
Fast enough for video calls, streaming, and remote work.

**Is there a TV?**  
Yes — a smart TV. You can log into your own streaming accounts.

**What if I lose the key?**  
There's a $50 lost key fee. To avoid this, always return the key to the lockbox when you go out — even for short trips.

**What if I need to cancel?**  
See our cancellation policy: 30+ days = full refund minus processing fee, 14–29 days = 50%, under 14 = no refund. Life happens — reach out and we'll see what we can do.

**Who do I contact if something goes wrong?**  
Property Manager contact info is included in your check-in instructions. For emergencies, call 911.
